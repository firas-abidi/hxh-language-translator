How it works

Your text is first turned into Japanese kana, kanji are read phonetically using a built-in dictionary, and each kana is mapped to its glyph in the Hunter × Hunter syllabary. Because the chart covers all 71 base sounds (the full gojūon plus the voiced ゛ and semi-voiced ゜ marks), any Japanese word or phrase can be written in the HxH language. Contracted sounds like きゃ or しょ are written with their full kana (き+や, し+よ), and a long vowel (ー) uses the HxH language's own lengthening symbol, so no sound is ever dropped. 入力文字はかなに変換され、各かながHxH言語の文字に対応します。全71音をカバーしており、拗音（きゃ・しょ等）は大きなかなで、長音（ー）は専用の長音記号で表すため、音が欠けることはありません。
